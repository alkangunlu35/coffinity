Coffinity – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-16

This Privacy Policy explains how Coffinity collects, uses, stores, and protects user information when you use the Coffinity mobile application, website, and related services.

1. Who We Are

Coffinity is a digital platform designed to help users discover coffee-related products, brands, content, venues, and social experiences, including community and meet-up features.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use Coffinity, we may collect the following information:

  • Name, email address, and basic account information
  • Profile information you choose to provide
  • Authentication details through supported sign-in methods such as Google Sign-In
  • App and website usage data, preferences, interactions, and engagement information
  • Device-related technical information reasonably necessary for functionality, fraud prevention, and security
  • Content you submit, such as comments, ratings, reviews, profile details, messages, and meet-related content

3. How We Use Information

We may use your information to:

  • Create, maintain, and manage your account
  • Provide, operate, and improve the Coffinity experience
  • Personalize content, recommendations, and product discovery
  • Enable user interaction, meet-up features, and community participation
  • Maintain security, detect abuse, investigate misuse, and enforce our Terms
  • Analyze product performance and improve our services
  • Respond to support requests, complaints, legal notices, and data requests

Coffinity may allow users to submit comments, reviews, descriptions, ratings, and other content. Such content may be visible to other users or third parties depending on the feature.

Users are solely responsible for the content they create and publish. Coffinity does not guarantee the accuracy, legality, or reliability of user-submitted content.

Coffinity may include social or meet-up style features that enable users to discover or connect with others. Coffinity provides the digital platform only and does not verify all users, supervise in-person interactions, or guarantee the conduct, identity, intentions, compatibility, or safety of any user.

Any communication or meeting between users is undertaken at the users’ own risk.

6. Brand, Product, and Listing Information

Coffinity may display information about coffee brands, products, venues, and related content. Some of this information may come from user submissions, automated systems, public sources, partners, or editorial input. As a result, some content may be incomplete, outdated, or inaccurate.

We do not guarantee that all brand, product, or venue information is complete or error-free.

Coffinity is not responsible for product quality, order fulfillment, shipping, returns, refunds, payment issues, third-party terms, or any dispute arising from third-party purchases or services.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share limited information with:

  • Service providers that help operate Coffinity, such as hosting, authentication, analytics, or communications providers
  • Legal authorities, if required by law or reasonably necessary to protect rights, safety, users, or compliance

9. Data Retention

We retain data for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, maintain security, and enforce our agreements.

10. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user information. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
